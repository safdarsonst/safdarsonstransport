About Us

Founded by Safdar, who brings extensive experience in logistics and transport operations across the Middle East, Europe, and the UK, Safdar & Sons Transport Ltd was built on a foundation of reliability, efficiency, and dedication to customer satisfaction.

We operate a modern fleet consisting exclusively of Scania and Mercedes-Benz vehicles—brands renowned for their durability and performance. This ensures that every delivery is handled with the highest level of reliability and professionalism.

At Safdar & Sons, we take great pride in the service we provide and in the strong relationships we’ve built with our clients. Our mission is simple: to deliver every load safely, efficiently, and on time—every time.
